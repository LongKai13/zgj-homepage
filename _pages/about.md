## About

I am an Associate Professor (tenure-track) at the [John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science](https://jhc.sjtu.edu.cn/) at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/). I received my Ph.D. from the [College of Information Sciences and Technology](https://ist.psu.edu/) at [Penn State](https://www.psu.edu/) in 2020, where I was advised by [Prof. Zhenhui (Jessie) Li](https://jessielzh.com/). Before that, I earned my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2015.My work has been recognized by the **ECML-PKDD 2020 Best Applied Data Science Paper Award**. Besides, I was also awarded **Baidu AI Chinese Rising Stars Top 100** and the **ACM SIGSPATIAL China Rising Star in Spatial Intelligence**.

My research deeply roots in the complex systems of urban environments and transportation. I am particularly interested in building AI methods that can understand, represent, and interact with urban spatio-temporal environments.
