With a strong commitment to teaching and student development, I have devoted substantial effort to undergraduate education. I also serve as the deputy head of the Data Structures teaching group. My contributions to teaching and mentoring have been recognized with honors including the “Jiahe” Outstanding Teaching Award, the distinction of “Chair of Undergraduate Public Fundamental Courses”, Shanghai Jiao Tong University’s “Kaiyuan” Top Ten Teacher Award, and the Shanghai Jiao Tong University Outstanding Class Advisor Award.
